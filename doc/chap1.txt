  
  [1X1 [33X[0;0YIntroduction[133X[101X
  
  [33X[0;0YThis  package, [10XSL2Reps[110X, provides methods for constructing and testing matrix
  presentations of the representations of [23X\mathrm{SL}_2(\mathbb{Z})[123X.[133X
  
  [33X[0;0YIrreducible   representations   of   prime-power   level   are   constructed
  individually  by means of Weyl representations, and from these a list of all
  representations of a given degree or level may be produced.[133X
  
  
  [1X1.1 [33X[0;0YInstallation[133X[101X
  
  [33X[0;0YTo  install  [10XSL2Reps[110X,  first download it from [10XTODO[110X, then place it in the [10Xpkg[110X
  subdirectory  of  your  GAP  installation (or in the [10Xpkg[110X subdirectory of any
  other GAP root directory, for example one added with the [10X-l[110X argument).[133X
  
  [33X[0;0Y[10XSL2Reps[110X is then loaded with the GAP command[133X
  
  [33X[0;0Y[10Xgap> LoadPackage( "SL2Reps" );[110X[133X
